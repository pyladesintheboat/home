An independent press publishing radical, experimental, homosexual books for Guy Hocquenghem.

"This is how things were, says Callicratidas, between Orestes and Pylades, about whom it was traditional to wonder, as in the case of Achilles and Patroclus, who was the lover and who the beloved. Pylades was the lover, it seems. But as they grew older, and when the time of trial came—the two friends had to decide which one would face death—the beloved behaved as the lover. One should see a model in this... In this masculine affection, the one who had been loved 'gives love in return,' and to such an extent that it becomes difficult to know 'which of the two is the erastes'; the affection of the one who loves is returned to him by the beloved the way an image is reflected in a mirror."
